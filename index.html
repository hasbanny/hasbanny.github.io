About

Who am I? Hi! I'm Hasbanny! I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in business management. I am currently pursuing a master's degree at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley, where I study computer science with a focus on data science and machine learning algorithms.

What are my interests? I'm interested in exploring how to use technology to improve the lives of minoritarian communities, such as the undocumented/DACA community.

What do I do? I enjoy participating in projects that align directly with my interests. Currently, I am part of the Dream STEM Initiative, where I teach programming to undocumented youth and encourage them to pursue a college education. In addition, I am a web developer for Rise First, where I advocated for the inclusion of resources specfically designed for undocumented/DACA students and pitched the idea to add additional web filters to facilitate the access to these resources.
